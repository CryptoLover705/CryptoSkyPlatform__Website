Qrax Going to Make Strong Official Partnership With CryptoSky Platform

Hello Community, We delighted to notify you, Qrax is going to Make Official Partnership with CryptoSky Platform. QRAX is a network of confidential payments and a repository of cryptocurrency assets. Decentralized, peer-to-peer, quantum secure, anonymous, open source, focused on the development of the crypto community. We will provide strong support as much as possible and hopefully CS TEAM everthing will be cover.Best Wishes For QRAX Team. Details Below Here :

ABOUT

The advanced Proof of Stake algorithm QRAX is one of the most secure, energy efficient and decentralized blockchains. Anyone with QRAX coins can participate in ensuring the safety and health of the network and receive rewards.

Protect your assets with Confidential Transactions

  • QRAX provides the tools and resources to keep you in control of your funds at all times.
  • QRAX rewards everyone who stores coins in their wallets and keeps the network safe and healthy.
  • The balance of your coins is available for transactions at any time. There is no censorship or blocking in the decentralized and anonymous cryptocurrency QRAX.
  • Keep your identity, addresses and balances private if you wish or need to.
INNOVATIONS
    Advanced Proof of Stake
  • Get rewards simply by storing QRAX in your wallet. There is no need to purchase expensive mining equipment.
    • Masternodes
  • You can participate in the development and management of the QRAX ecosystem. Earn rewards without transferring control of your wallet and private keys to anyone.
    • Confidentiality with non-disclosure of transaction data
  • Send and receive QRAX as untracked transactions without showing anyone your balance, addresses, and transaction amounts.
    • DPoS - Delegated PoS
  • Safely store your assets by delegating coins to the online staking masternode and earning you rewards.
    • Sustainable Economy
  • The QRAX monetary system has a long-term balance between inflation and deflation, providing scalability, resilience and decentralized control.
    • Decentralized Governance
  • Proposals put forward for the formation of budgetary expenditures for development are approved by the community and are voted on by masternodes.
