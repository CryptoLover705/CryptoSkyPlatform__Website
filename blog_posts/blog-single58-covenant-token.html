Covenant Token made Official Partnership With CryptoSky Platform

Hello Crypto Supporters, We are glad to inform you that, Covenant Token made Official Partnership with our platform. Covenant Token is a Faith-Based token. A share of our token supply is reserved for our Ministry partners and Charitable Organizations globally. Details bellow ...

ABOUT

God wants us to succeed, to be prosperous and to fill the earth. Wealth is not evil but rather the harm that is done by turning away from God and using wealth to do evil. Covenant Token is unique as the first faith-based token on the Binance Smart Chain. A share of our token supply is reserved for our partner ministries and charitable organizations globally. We are now bringing this formula to other chains with the launch of Worship, the first multi-chain faith based token. Worship will launch on November 28, 2021 on Ethereum Mainnet, Binance Smart Chain, Polygon, KCC, xDai, Avalanche, Fantom, and Harmony.
