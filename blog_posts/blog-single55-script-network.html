ABOUT

Script TV is built on Script blockchain – which is a reliable, affordable, high-quality video publishing platform using our own technology. Using a decentralized infrastructure, Script TV provides a video platform tailored to what current incumbent content applications need. Script.TV will be the first linear tv and film experience on blockchain, so it solves shortcomings that millions have disliked for decades within the centralized media space including low cpms due to middlemen, slow payouts, delays in launches and a lack of analytical data (content partners side). From a users side, there is no ability to get paid for what they watch (infact users are normally forced to pay to watch content), alongside no opportunity to connect with the current community or the content partners. For us, the tv and film experience is broken, and our aim is to change that.

Lets simplify this. Ultimately, Script TV is a two sided platform: one for content partners who have their shows and/or films streaming on the platform and the viewer (users) enjoys it by viewing them from anywhere in the globe.

Content partners earn $SPAY Tokens for every minute their content is watched on Script TV by users
