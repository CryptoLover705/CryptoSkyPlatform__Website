ABOUT

DeFi is a fast-growing sector of the cryptocurrency industry. The vast majority of DeFi applications are built on the Ethereum blockchain, but Ethereumâ€™s limited throughput and high transaction fees prevent DeFi from being adopted on a large scale. Tofu Project goal is to address this issue by utilizing the TRON blockchain. Our solution is to create simple and fast bridge between two blockchains to allow people trade wrapped ERC20 tokens on TRON with low fees. And on the other hand we want to make possible to trade wrapped TRC20 tokens on Ethereum blockchain which also can be useful in some cases. We call it TofuBridge.

TofuSwap is a protocol for automated token exchange on the TRON network. TofuSwap based on the same idea as Uniswap or JustSwap, but with some modifications and improvements. It will be integrated with TofuBridge.
