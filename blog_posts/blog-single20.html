ABOUT

Polkamoon is a decentralised deflationary currency on the Ethereum network inspired by Polkadot that rewards long-term holders with an ever rising price floor due to its tokenomics.

We wanted to provide a community-led token with encoded permanent liquidity, holding incentives and its own NFT platform.

Polkamoon is launched in three phases with presale being distributed in batches so as to protect holders from initial price volatility Tokenomics

The original Polkamoon supply was 770,000,000,000,000. Each time a Polkamoon is transferred 15% of the transacted amount is burned. 3% goes into liquidity, 2% to holders. There will never be newly minted Polkamoon.

Liquidity is initially locked (see unicrypt) for a minimum of 1 month. Supply from transactions will increase the pool size.
