QBC International Has Been Listed On CryptoSky Platform

Hello Everyone,
We are glad to inform you, QBC International Now Successfully Listed On Our Platform. Quebecoin is an experimental digital currency that enables instant payments to anyone, anywhere in the world. Quebecoin uses peer-to-peer technology to operate with no central authority: managing transactions and issuing money are carried out collectively by the network. Quebecoin Core is the name of open source software which enables the use of this currency.. Below Project Details.....

ABOUT



Québecoin was launched in April 2014 and abandoned in the subsequent few days afterward. After it was abandoned, enterprising individuals attempted a relaunch starting in March 2018 transferring QBC to a new chain based on DASH and the X11 algorithm which was especially popular at the time and remains in use today by many coins.

Québecoin appeared to be recovering and the community grew internationally but progress was destroyed when in October 2018 2nd Québecoin was targeted by a 51% attack and Québecoin lost its main exchange (YoBit). The sudden loss of confidence and liquidity meant activity rapidly decreased as the main supporters and developers abandoned the project in Quebec itself. With essentially no community supporting QBC, today's current supporters, who were just a ragtag crew of a few friends that knew each other from other crypto commuities, proved what open source really means deciding in late 2018 to reactivate the 2nd chain.

By early 2019 the team realized in order to restore confidence in Québecoin, they needed to build a new chain with multiple algorithms to maximize the potential of the 20 second blocktime and ensure security. The active members of the QBC community then started the process of securing the chain , forking to the current X7 algorithm chain.

This was done by a hard fork that was created from myriad (MYR) to form QBC's 3rd chain. The current chain was started May 13, 2019. The swap from the older X11 chain was completed on July 13.2019 and the chain of the 2nd Québecoin was successfully phased out.

QBC is now based on X7 multi algorithm: X7 includes SHA256/Scrypt/Groestl/Skein/Yescrypt/Argon2d algorithms

Quebecoin‐QBC is fully open source. It is currently maintained by a international community that primarily uses its Discord as a meeting platform.

QBC is now under continuous development by it's current main developer CryptoLover. He is updating the codebase that will remove the 50% dev fee QBC has today and implement a small maintenence fee for the running expenses like domain, explorer etc. Our main developer is also updating all types of wallet platforms. He has also completed necessary code to add QBC to Trezor and Ledger hardware wallets.
