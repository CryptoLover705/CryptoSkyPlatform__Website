Global Adversity Made A Official Partnership With CryptoSky Platform

Hello Audience, We are delighted to inform you that, Global Adversity made a Official Partnership with CryptoSky Platform. Global Adversity Project (GAP) is a yield-generating charitable cryptocurrency that runs on the Binance Smart Network. It rewards holders by providing them ‘interest’ on their investment through the contract transaction tax, offering an incentive for investors to hold, rather then participating in day-trading events.

Furthermore, investors will be supporting a Global Charitable Cause, one that seeks to help those who may struggle with a disability, whether it be mental, physical or intellectual based. Details Below Here :

ABOUT

The idea for the Global Adversity Project was originally founded in May 2021.
After investing in numerous tokens throughout their time, the GAP Team found themselves investing more and more in charity related tokens, as they served a dual purpose. First, and foremost, they had a good cause behind them and second, it allowed them to make investments into something that was trying to make a difference in the world.

Between the meme tokens and the charity tokens, it was noticed that there were certain areas that just weren’t being represented and that they felt were quite important. With several members of the team working with individuals who have disabilities, having family members with a disability or simply knowing someone with a disability, it was a place that touched ‘home’ for them.

But why the Global Adversity Project? Well, one of the definitions of ‘adversity’ is ‘difficulties’, it’s a ‘project’ and they want it to be ‘global’. Individuals with disabilities, whether they be mental, physical or intellectual, face more difficulties in the world then those who may not have a disability.

According to the World Health Organization (WHO), people with disabilities are among the most marginalized groups in the world. They typically have poorer health care, lower education achievements, less economic participation and higher rates of poverty. What’s surprising about this is that over 1 billion people live with some form of a disability. That’s an astonishing 15% of the world’s population.

It’s for this very reason that the Global Adversity Project was founded. While we know that we will never be able to change the world, we want to be able to have an impact and to provide a better opportunity for those who face difficulties in their everyday life due to a disability. Through our Charity Wallet, the GAP Fund, we want to be able to make donations to organizations that represent these individuals. In the future, we want to establish the GAP Education Fund, in order to offer grants/bursaries to individuals with disabilities who may be seeking to attend college, university or trade school.

We fully believe that the Global Adversity Project is going to be able to make a difference in the future and with that, we believe that it will be a great opportunity for investors to invest their money into. We will never be looking for ‘pump and dump’ schemes and will be focusing on organic and steady growth through our marketing strategies.

World Health Organization Source: https://www.who.int/features/factfiles/disability/en/
